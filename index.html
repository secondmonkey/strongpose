details

Several studies demonstrate that assuming open and expansive postures for as few as 2 minutes can dramatically improve performance in stressful social situations1,2.

1 Carney et al, 2010 Compared to poses that convey weakness, strength poses lead to measurable increases in testosterone and reductions in cortisol.

2 Cuddy et al, 2012 (working paper) Significant improvements in performance on a stressful task following the same poses.
